About Me

I am a wizard of numbers and a sorcerer of data. I have a passion for delving into the hidden intricacies of dynamical systems and advancing the field of artificial intelligence.

AI Researcher, Investor & Swing Trader

I worked for my undergraduate degree in Data Science at IIT Madras and advanced my education with degrees in Mathematics from IIT Delhi. Throughout my research career, I have had the privilege of being at esteemed institutions such as IIT Roorkee and IIT Delhi. I have been fortunate to receive several prestigious fellowships, including the NBHM Postdoc Fellowship in 2022 and the SPM-CSIR Research Fellowship in 2019, which have supported my research endeavors. I have accomplished a number of notable academic feats, including ranking first in the IIT GATE 2020, the CSIR NET 2019, and the IIT JAM 2015 exams. I am proud to have received several other awards including the NBHM Research Fellowship in 2018, the NBHM Masters Fellowship in 2016, and the IIT Delhi Institute Research Fellowship in 2017.
