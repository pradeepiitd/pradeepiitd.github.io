About Me

I am a Quant

As a seasoned professional with an innate knack for mathematics and data science, I am driven to excel in the field of AI and finance. My passion for analytics and deep understanding of these domains have propelled me to expand my portfolio and deliver innovative solutions. With a strategic mindset and an unwavering commitment to achieving success, I approach each challenge with a rigorous analytical approach, blending it with a strategic vision to make a positive impact in both arenas.

I have had an illustrious academic journey, spanning seven years at IITD and four years at IITM, culminating in advanced degrees in Mathematics and Data Science. Throughout my research career, I have had the privilege of being at esteemed institutions such as IITR and IITD, receiving several prestigious fellowships such as the NBHM Postdoc Fellowship in 2022 and the SPM-CSIR Research Fellowship in 2019, which have supported my research endeavours. My accomplishments include ranking first in the IIT GATE 2020, the CSIR NET 2019, and the IIT JAM 2015 exams, as well as receiving several other awards such as the NBHM Research Fellowship in 2018, the NBHM Masters Fellowship in 2016, and the IIT Delhi Institute Research Fellowship in 2017. With my training and expertise, I remain committed to upholding the highest standards of rigour and elegance as I navigate the financial markets and venture into new research frontiers.
