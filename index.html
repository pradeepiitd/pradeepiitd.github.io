Dr. S. Pradeep

Mathematician

With a strong affinity for the interdisciplinary interplay between AI and Mathematics, my expertise as a Quant fuels my pursuit of diverse high-dimensional problems and creative solutions. I wield a sagacious mindset and a relentless determination to achieve excellence. Each challenge I undertake is met with a meticulous analytical approach, expertly integrated with visionary foresight to effect a significant influence across both fields.

My academic journey, encompassing seven years at IIT D and four years at IIT M, furnished me with advanced degrees in Mathematics and Data Science. I've had the privilege of a research career that includes collaborations with prestigious institutions such as IIT R and IIT D, as well as receiving distinguished fellowships like the NBHM Postdoctoral Fellowship and the SPM-CSIR Research Fellowship. As I embark on new research endeavours and delve into the intricacies of novel Dynamical Systems, I remain steadfast in my commitment to upholding the zenith of intellectual rigour and elegance in my pursuits.
