Dr. S. Pradeep

I am a mathematician with a keen interest in the structures of dynamical systems and the emergence of intelligence. My primary affiliation is with the Machine Intelligence Lab, Department of CSE, IIT Roorkee, where I serve as a Post-Doctoral Researcher & Principal Investigator, specializing in symbolic systems and machine learning. My research on geometric deep learning is supported by the National Post-Doctoral Fellowship (N-PDF) from Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB), under the mentorship of Prof. R. Balasubramanian. In a prior life, I was a researcher at IIT Delhi, working under the mentorship of Prof. A. Nagar on dynamical systems & Riemannian geometry. My work there earned me the prestigious SPM fellowship from the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR), an accolade given to up to two national toppers annually. I hold a doctorate in symbolic systems and a master's degree in mathematics from IIT Delhi; and a bachelor's degree in data science from IIT Madras, all of which were funded by merit-based scholarships. I feel incredibly fortunate to have received these opportunities and remain deeply committed to contributing to these institutions. Beyond academics, I am proficient in financial analysis and possess ambidextrous abilities, including touch typing and writing with both hands, as well as playing sports with both feet, which enhances my adaptability in various tasks. Outside of my professional interests, I enjoy bicycling, literature, and soccer.
