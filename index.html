Essence

As a seasoned professional with an innate knack for mathematics and data science, I am driven to excel in the realms of AI and finance. My fervour for analytics and profound comprehension of these fields have inspired me to broaden my expertise and deliver innovative solutions. Possessing a strategic mindset and steadfast dedication to success, I tackle every challenge through a methodical analytical lens, harmoniously blending it with strategic foresight to effectuate meaningful progress in both spheres.

Over the course of an illustrious academic odyssey spanning seven years at IIT Delhi and four years at IIT Madras, I have garnered advanced degrees in Mathematics and Data Science. My research career has been graced by associations with esteemed institutions such as IIT Roorkee and IIT Delhi, receiving coveted fellowships like the NBHM Postdoctoral Fellowship in 2022 and the SPM-CSIR Research Fellowship in 2019. My accomplishments encompass an array of prestigious rankings, including first place in IIT GATE 2020, CSIR NET 2019, and IIT JAM 2015 exams, as well as numerous accolades, such as the NBHM Research Fellowship in 2018, NBHM Masters Fellowship in 2016, and IIT Delhi Institute Research Fellowship in 2017. As I embark on new research endeavours and delve into the intricacies of financial markets, I remain steadfast in my commitment to upholding the zenith of intellectual rigour and elegance in my pursuits.
