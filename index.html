Dr. S. Pradeep

Mathematician/Quant

As a connoisseur of mathematics and data science, I am ardently committed to thriving within the spheres of AI and finance. My zeal for analytics and an acute acumen in these domains have galvanized my pursuit to cultivate a diverse portfolio and devise avant-garde solutions. Equipped with a sagacious mentality and an unwavering resolve to attain success, I address each challenge utilizing a meticulous analytical methodology, seamlessly amalgamating it with visionary foresight to engender a profound impact across both disciplines.

During my academic journey spanning seven years at IIT D and four years at IIT M, I have earned advanced degrees in Mathematics and Data Science. My research career includes collaborations with reputable institutions such as IIT R and IIT D, as well as receiving distinguished fellowships like the NBHM Postdoctoral Fellowship in 2022 and the SPM-CSIR Research Fellowship in 2019. My achievements include a range of impressive rankings, including first place in IIT GATE 2020, CSIR NET 2019, and IIT JAM 2015 exams, as well as various awards, such as the NBHM Research Fellowship in 2018, NBHM Masters Fellowship in 2016, and IIT Delhi Institute Research Fellowship in 2017. As I embark on new research endeavours and delve into the intricacies of financial markets, I remain steadfast in my commitment to upholding the zenith of intellectual rigour and elegance in my pursuits.
