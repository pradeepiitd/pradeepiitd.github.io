Dr. S. Pradeep

I am a mathematician with a penchant for the intricacies of Dynamical Systems and their connection with Artificial Intelligence. My primary affiliation is with the Machine Intelligence Lab, within the Department of CSE at IIT Roorkee, where I currently serve as a Post-Doctoral Researcher & Principal Investigator, specializing in Dynamical Systems, Machine Learning, and AI. My current research is supported by the prestigious NBHM fellowship from Department of Atomic Energy, under the mentorship of Prof. R. Balasubramanian.

Before this, I've had an enriching period of research at IIT Delhi, where I dived deep into the world of Dynamical Systems & Riemannian Geometry, mentored by Prof. A. Nagar. My achievements during this time were recognized with the SPM fellowship from Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, an accolade given to up to two national toppers annually. I earned my PhD and Masters in Mathematics from IIT Delhi, and undertook undergraduate studies in Data Science at IIT Madras. My proficiencies extend to ambidexterity, application development, financial analysis, maintaining mathematical rigour, dexterous touch typing, and adroit trading. On a more personal note, I have a fondness for bicycling, literature, and soccer.
